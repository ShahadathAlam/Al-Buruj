About Us

Established in Nov 25, 2020, Al-Buruj Institute caters for only men in providing classical Islamic education. Our vision is to provide strong foundations in Arabic and to revolutionise the acquisition of sacred knowledge, making it accessible to everyone.

The foundation of our educational resources primarily rests upon the esteemed contributions of the Al-Qalam Institute. Their materials constitute the cornerstone of our curriculum, offering a wealth of knowledge and insights that are highly regarded within our academic community. These resources, meticulously curated and continuously updated by experts in the field, serve as a beacon of excellence, guiding our students on a path of comprehensive learning and intellectual growth. Through our partnership with the Al-Qalam Institute, we are proud to provide students with access to a vast and reputable repository of study materials, fostering a deep and meaningful educational journey.


What makes us unique?
--Our focus is to provide strong foundations in Arabic as an essential base for Alimiyyah studies.
-- We aim to equip students with the tools to access the Quran, Hadith and Arabic material, confidently and independently.

We continuously strive in seeking the pleasure of our Lord, Most High, inculcating the Sunnah of His Beloved Messenger ﷺ , attaining excellence and providing enthusiasm for seeking knowledge and serving mankind.
